Welcome to PC Show Off




At PC Show Off, we believe that every computer rig is unique and deserves to be shared with the world. Create a profile and showcase your PC by selecting each component from our comprehensive database. Can't find your exact part? Don't worry, you can submit a request to add it to our database!

How it works:

  1. Create a profile to get started.
  2. Complete the online form for your PC build by choosing components from our list.
  3. If you can't find a specific part, submit a form to add it to our database.
  4. Your requested component will be reviewed by our staff before appearing in the list.
  5. Show off your PC build to the world!

Join the PC Show Off community today and start sharing your unique computer rig with other enthusiasts!
